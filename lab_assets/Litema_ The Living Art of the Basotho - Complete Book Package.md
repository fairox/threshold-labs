# Litema: The Living Art of the Basotho - Complete Book Package

## Overview

This package contains the complete content for a 100-page coffee table book on Litema, the traditional mural art of the Basotho people. The book is designed for art enthusiasts, academics, and cultural preservationists, featuring a visual-heavy format with 60% images and 40% text.

### 1. Main Book Content
