# Brand Guidelines: Litema Book Project

## 1. Color Palette
The palette is derived from natural earth pigments but applied with CMYK precision.
- **Ochre Red:** HEX #B2422E (Symbolizing "Letsoku" / Blood of the Earth)
- **Carbon Black:** HEX #1A1A1B (Symbolizing Charcoal / Rain Clouds)
- **Clay White:** HEX #F9F7F2 (Symbolizing Purity / Ancestors) - *Note: Use this as the paper base color instead of pure white.*
- **Mountain Slate:** HEX #4A4E54 (Accent for historical photos)

## 2. Typography
- **Primary Header (Display):** *Inter Bold* or *Montserrat ExtraBold*. Use for Chapter titles. Letter spacing: -0.05em.
- **Secondary Header:** *Inter Medium*. All caps. Letter spacing: 0.2em.
- **Body Text:** *Adobe Caslon Pro* or *Sabon*. (A classic serif provides a warm, human contrast to the cold grid).
- **Captions/Folios:** *Inter Regular* at 7pt.

## 3. Graphic Elements
- **Rules:** Use 0.5pt black horizontal and vertical lines to define the grid (Reference: Image 11).
- **Iconography:** Use simplified, vector-style Litema motifs (triangles, zig-zags) as page breakers or corner folios.

## 4. Photography Style
- **Macro:** Deep focus on the texture of dried mud and dung.
- **Portraits:** High-contrast Black & White, emphasizing the weathered hands of the artists (Reference: Image 4).
- **Landscapes:** Desaturated colors to let the vibrant Litema on the houses pop.
